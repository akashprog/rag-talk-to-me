# Akash Jain — Professional Profile

## Early life and education

Akash Jain was born and raised in Delhi, India. He completed his schooling in 2009, 
at Ryan International School, Delhi, where he was an active participant 
across academics, sports, and extracurricular activities. Akash played 
cricket, practiced taekwondo, competed in skating, participated in marathons, 
and was a regular in school debates.

Akash went on to study Bachelor of Technology in Information Technology at 
Bharati Vidyapeeth College of Engineering in Pune, Maharashtra, India, 
from 2009 to 2013, graduating with distinction.

## Academic highlights and leadership

During his time at Ryan International School, Akash was elected Sports 
Minister for the school's students' parliament — a structured initiative 
designed to teach students how a real parliament functions, including 
campaigning, building consensus across opposing views, and taking on 
administrative responsibility. Akash campaigned across two slots and won 
the post through a verbal vote. As Sports Minister, he led the sports 
activities for the annual school fest, managing a small team of two 
fellow students to handle logistics outside school hours.

At Bharati Vidyapeeth College of Engineering, Akash was elected Marketing 
Head of the IT department for the annual fest in 2012. He led a team of 
juniors to bring in sponsorships for the college fest.

## How I got into data and technology

Akash always had a strong inclination towards programming, excelling in 
practical exams across processor programming, C, C++, Java, and data 
structures. His approach was always first-principles — not content with 
knowing how something worked in theory, he consistently asked where and 
how it was applied in practice. This deepened his interest in building 
real systems.

During college, Akash interned at Antenna Software in Bangalore, where he 
designed an attendance management system for office employees, built on a 
three-tier architecture. It was his first fully functional application and 
significantly deepened his interest in IT systems.

His final college project focused on Web 3.0 and the semantic web. Akash 
designed a web application with a dictionary of words and meanings, where 
a search engine would interpret the meaning of a search term and surface 
images tagged with matching descriptions. The project was well received 
and marked a turning point in his interest in intelligent systems.

## Career timeline

Akash began his career as a Software Engineer (Java) at Yodlee Infotech 
in Bangalore, India from October 2013 to April 2015. He then joined 
Infosys in Pune as a Systems Engineer from April 2015 to March 2016.

In June 2016, Akash joined Knowesis in Singapore, where he progressed 
rapidly from Software Engineer through Delivery Lead and Solutions Architect 
to Technical Presales, a role he held until August 2022. He was promoted 
within two years to lead delivery initiatives — a recognition of consistent 
performance.

In August 2022, Akash joined Gulf Business Machines (GBM) in Abu Dhabi, UAE 
as a Senior Solutions Architect. He was promoted to Lead Architect within 
2.5 years and continues in this role. He has received consistent annual 
performance ratings of 4.5 and above.

## Key engagements and projects

At Telstra in Australia (2016–2017), Akash designed the integration layer 
using Apache Camel and led the deployment activities for a major platform 
migration while at Knowesis.

At Batelco in Bahrain (2017–2018), Akash led the full implementation of 
the Knowesis product, managing delivery end to end.

At Etisalat in the UAE (2018), Akash led a successful proof of concept 
demonstrating the core capabilities of the Knowesis product. The engagement 
resulted in Knowesis being awarded the project.

At e& (formerly Etisalat) in the UAE (2022–2023), Akash led the big data 
unification project at GBM, one of his first major engagements after joining.

## What I offer

Akash works as a hands-on solutions consultant specialising in real-time 
data, big data, and AI solutions. He is available for advisory engagements, 
solution architecture, technical presales support, and proof of concept 
design. His engagements typically span enterprise clients in banking, 
telecommunications, and government sectors across the Gulf region and 
globally. He brings both the technical depth to design solutions and the 
communication skills to present them to executive stakeholders.

## How I work

Akash starts every engagement by clearly defining the customer's problem — 
often working with the customer to reframe or sharpen it before any solution 
is proposed. He believes a well-defined problem is more valuable than a 
premature solution. From there he curates solutions that are grounded in 
the customer's actual constraints — technical, organisational, and commercial. 
He is hands-on by default, comfortable going deep into architecture and code 
while also operating at the executive level when needed.
